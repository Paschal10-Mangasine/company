Registration of Business Names and Companies

We legally register business names and companies, and we process up to obtaining a certificate as well as a business license, and our costs are very cheap.

Website and application development.

We develop dynamic and static websites as well as applications (Programs) for schools, companies, hospitals, colleges, institutions such as churches, we also develop systems for selling products of stores and various activities related to the sale of products online.

We provide online document printing service

We offer this online document printing service in all stationeries, especially in college areas to help students print their documents faster and more easily, to save their time for other activities. So this service is good and its costs are very cheap and it helps the user as well as the owner of the stationery in the collection of his income.

Internet service (ISP)

We provide internet service in offices, institutions, schools, homes, we also provide internet service in the streets, so we care about the needs of customers and decide to reach customers in all ways, in order to promote the national economy in a technological way.

Marketing and distribution of food

We provide food retail service as well as general production, so we distribute food grains to institutions such as schools, orphanages, as well as various places, we also distribute food to various activities such as weddings, celebrations, tragedies and so on. We also sell ready-to-eat foods, by special order through our application program for ordering food and fruit called MANGASINE MEAL, where the specific goal of this program is to help the community serve food especially to office workers to help them deliver safely. and healthy food.

Video production service and live streaming service

We provide videography services for various events such as tourist trips, celebrations such as weddings, celebrations, send-off ceremonies, Blessings, as well as tragedies or funerals. We also broadcast live events through social networks such as YouTube, Facebook, Instagram and Tiktok at an affordable cost.

Song and music recording services

We provide music recording services including gospel songs, from single songs to albums at affordable prices for all people.
